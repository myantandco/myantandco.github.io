More About Us.

We’ve been getting a lot of questions about what we’re up to, so here’s a brief summary of what we’re building and some answers to commonly asked questions.

First, some context. Some time ago, we started working on a wearable slightly different than the ones you’re used to seeing today. When we set out to build it, we had a few pre-requisites tied to our dissatisfaction with what is currently available in the market. We labeled these our “required characteristics.”

Required Characteristics

Pre-Existing Adoption

Our wearable needed to have pre-existing adoption (we should already be buying the house that our wearables live in)

Friction Free

Our wearable should be friction free (you shouldn’t even know it’s there)

Short Renweal Cycle

Our wearable should have a renewal cycle of no more than one year.

Demographic Agnostic

It should be demographic agnostic. (whether you’re 14 yrs or 65 yrs, it should be accessible to you)

Holistic

It needed to be holistic, not activity specific.

Growth

Lastly, it needed to identify areas of growth and opportunity.

End Result

We’re happy with what we’ve been able to create so far given this vision, and we’re very close to announcing it to the world. By signing up now, you are joining a community of our earliest users. Building something like this requires A LOT of diversity in people, lifestyles, activities, and scenarios. Our plan is to start reaching out to people we identify as ideal early users.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does it mean to be one of Kiin’s early human guinea pigs?

Guinea pig basically means beta users. We need a diversity of people from all walks of life to build something great. If you’ve signed up on the kiin website, then we may reach out to you to ask you to participate in some testing. Participation is completely optional.

What is so different about your wearable?

We’ve been perpetually let down by promises of single products changing and optimizing our lives through the collection of bio-metric data. We think there are a few critical elements that have led to this failure (our required characteristics among them). Without giving away too much, our product addresses these critical elements.
